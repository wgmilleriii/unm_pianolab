# UNM Piano Lab Dante Pilot - Amazon Shopping List

This list contains the components required for the 4-station proof-of-concept (1 Teacher + 3 Students).

## Core Dante Hardware
*   **4 × OREI Dante XLR Encoder (Transmitter) - DXA-XLR-TX**
    *   [Search on Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=OREI+DXA-XLR-TX+Dante+XLR+Encoder)
    *   *Purpose: Connects Piano audio output to the Dante network.*
*   **4 × OREI Dante XLR Decoder (Receiver) - DXA-XLR-RX**
    *   [Search on Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=OREI+DXA-XLR-RX+Dante+XLR+Decoder)
    *   *Purpose: Receives audio from the Dante network for headphones.*

## Monitoring & Distribution
*   **4 × Behringer Microamp HA400 4-Channel Headphone Amplifier**
    *   [Search on Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Behringer+HA400)
    *   *Purpose: Provides headphone outputs with individual volume control at each station.*

## Networking
*   **1 × 24-Port Gigabit PoE+ Switch (e.g., TP-Link TL-SG1428PE or TL-SG1218MP)**
    *   [Search on Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=24+port+gigabit+poe+switch+tp-link)
    *   *Purpose: Central hub providing data and power (PoE) to Dante units.*

## Cables & Adapters
*   **4 × 1/4" TRS Stereo to Dual XLR Male Breakout Cable (10ft)**
    *   [Search on Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=1%2F4+TRS+to+Dual+XLR+Male+Breakout+Cable+10ft)
    *   *Purpose: Connects Piano Headphone/Output to the Dante Transmitter.*
*   **4 × Dual XLR Female to 1/4" TRS Male Cable (or 2x XLR-F to 1/4" TRS-M)**
    *   [Search on Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=XLR+Female+to+1%2F4+TRS+Male+Cable)
    *   *Purpose: Connects Dante Receiver outputs to the HA400 Headphone Amp input.*
*   **Ethernet Patch Cables (Cat6)**
    *   [Search on Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Cat6+Ethernet+Patch+Cables+10-pack)
    *   *Purpose: Connects all stations to the PoE switch.*

---
**Note:** For the initial $300 experiment recommended in the README, purchase only **one** of each item (except the switch, which is needed for any networking).
